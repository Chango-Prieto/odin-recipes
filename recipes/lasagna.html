Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, is a type of pasta made in wide, flat sheets. It originates in Italian cuisine, where it is served in a number of ways.

Ingredients

  • 9 Lasagna sheets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 4 cans (15 ounce) of tomato sauce
  • 1 can (16 ounce) sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
